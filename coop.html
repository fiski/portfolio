Coop Insights

Coop Insights is a self service platform for Coop Sverige AB's b2b clients where they can follow their sales in real time and get unique customer insights

Intro

Challenge

Coop Sverige AB's outdated supplier portal suffered from low user adoption due to its difficult interface and lack of actionable insights. This resulted in missed sales opportunities for both Coop and their suppliers. The goal was to create Coop Insights - a modern platform that would help suppliers identify trends, track real-time sales data, and make informed decisions based on customer behavior.

Key Deliverable

Wireframes

Through multiple iterations and stakeholder feedback, wireframes helped identify which features should be included in the MVP and what could be developed later. The process explored responsive layouts across desktop, tablet, and smartphone breakpoints, ensuring a consistent experience across devices.

User Research

Admin interviews

Interviews were held together with one of the current admins to identify which features they are currently using on the previous portal and determine what is essential. The specific admin was updated weekly about what was going on in the project.
