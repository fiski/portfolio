Auctionet

Display distance between yourself to the auction.

A UX issue on Auctionet is poor search accuracy. I’ll never bid on distant auctions, yet I’m stuck scrolling and Googling locations. My Chrome extension shows distance to auction houses with custom filters.

GitHub link

https://github.com/fiski/auctionet

Tradera & Vinted

Filter to exclude unwanted items.

A UX problem on Tradera and Vinted is no exclude filters. I’ll never buy a fast-fashion blazer, but I’m forced to scroll through pages of them, getting annoyed by page 14 of 22.

GitHub link

https://github.com/fiski/site-specific-scribe

BrandsFrom

A database of Swedish brands and manufacturers

Made in Sweden? It's not always as simple as it sounds. This database creates transparency about Swedish brands and where their products are actually manufactured.

Visit the website here

https://brandsfrom.se/
