Coop Insights

Coop Insights is a self service platform for Coop Sverige AB's b2b clients where they can follow their sales in real time and get unique customer insights

Solution

A highly curated experience

Coop Insights makes the process of tracking sales, noticing trends and taking action simple and engaging. It helps the admins workflow with writing articles, sending out information and engaging with the customers.
